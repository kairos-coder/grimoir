⟡ Grimoir Commands

  • relic Summon a random passage from the archive.
  • write [text] Inscribe new text into the grimoir.
  • find [text] Search the archive for passages containing your phrase. Matches are highlighted.
  • harvest Surface the 5 most recently inscribed passages, newest first.
  • help Show this command reference.
